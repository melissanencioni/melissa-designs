How I got here

I started experimenting with Photoshop when I was 12 and learned basic HTML and CSS back when we had Myspace profiles. I studied in Florence and Barcelona and obtained my bachelor in Visual Design in 2017.

I continued my education by studying UX/UI design and, after working for a few years as an in-house designer in Barcelona, in 2020 I decided to dive deeper into the world of web development and soon became a full-time freelancer. Today, I apply my experience and creativity to work with businesses and entrepreneurs.

I love working as a freelancer because each project is unique and pushes me to learn something new. The challenges of the fast changing design and tech world and the opportunities to engage directly with my clients keep me motivated and make me pour my heart into every project.

Webflow is my go-to platform to build websites, as it allows me to create completely customized designs and work efficiently.
