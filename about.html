I am a freelancer specialising in web design and development based in Barcelona, Spain.

How did I get here?

Iâ€™ve been into visual and techy things since I can remember: I used to draw a lot as a kid, I discovered Photoshop when I was 11 and started learning HTML and CSS when we still had Myspace profiles. I studied in Florence and Barcelona and got my bachelor degree in Visual Design in 2017.

My design journey began with editorial design: my final graduation project was a magazine focused on fashion and photography. Following that, I collaborated with a local print magazine for a few months and gradually transitioned to working exclusively on digital projects.

I continued my education by studying UX/UI design through various online certifications and independent study. After working for a few years as an in-house designer in Barcelona, when COVID-19 hit, I decided to dive deeper into the world of web development and became a full-time freelancer.

so here I am today!
