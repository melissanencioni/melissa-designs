You’re 6 steps away from a new website

Although I adapt the process to each individual project, this is usually my workflow to create a website from start to finish.

1. proposal

Let's start with a meeting to get to know each other and the details of your project, expectations and goals. Once the scope of the project is defined, I'll send you my proposal and estimate. We'll sign a contract based on what we have agreed.

2. Research

I'll start by gathering visual references that are relevant to the project and brainstorming ideas. This step is crucial for me to understand your preferences, so you should share anything that comes to your mind.

3. Content planning
& wireframes

Let's define the content and how to distribute it across pages and sections. I will create a basic structure (wireframes), propose different layouts and gather your feedback to refine them.

4. Visual design

I'll work on one or more design concepts and start applying visual design to the wireframes, we'll choose a style and refine it. At this point, you should provide your written content and all the elements on the page should be defined. I will pull everything together and create the final mockups.

5. Development

Once the design is signed-off, I will build the website following the mockups. I will add animations to bring the design to life and work on the functionalities you need. I will take care of technical SEO and launch settings.

6. Test, launch, tutorial

After a round of feedback and testing on the finished website, we'll be ready to lauch. I'll give you a tutorial to teach you to update content and make simple edits by yourself.
